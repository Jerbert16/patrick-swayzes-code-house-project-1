Star Gazer

Welcome to the Everyday Stargazer web app. Since antiquity, human beings have been looking up at the night sky and indulging their sense of wonder. Now, with our Everyday Stargazer web application, you too can entertain your own celestial curiosities.

The Everyday Stargazer makes it simple to get a map of the night sky. All you have to do is enter the name of your city, then select the date you wish to go stargazing and voila – you’ll be presented with current weather conditions and a star chart of your local night sky. Ready? Let’s gaze.
